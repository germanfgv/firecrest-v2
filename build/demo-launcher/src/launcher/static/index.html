FirecREST v2 Demo Launcher

Step 1: Set-up your SSH Credentials

The SSH credentials are required by FirecREST to access your cluster’s login node.
The credentials are only temporarily stored in the Docker container.

Step 2: Set-up your SSH Connection

Provide your cluster’s login node hostname and SSH port.
If a SSH tunneling is required please provide the proxy hostname and port.

Step 3: Setup your Scheduler

Enter your HPC cluster’s name and choose its scheduler type.

Step 4: Access your FirecREST playground

The FirecREST v2 demo instance is up and running!

Examples

The following are a set of curl examples you can copy and paste in your terminal to quicky interact with the FirecREST API.

Submit Job:
curl -X POST --header "Content-Type: application/json" --header "Authorization: Bearer " -d '{ "job": {"name": "Count to 100", "working_directory": "", "script": "#!/bin/bash\nfor i in {1..100}\ndo\necho $i\nsleep 1\ndone"}}' "http://localhost:8000/compute/cluster/jobs"
List Jobs:
curl --header "Authorization: Bearer " "http://localhost:8000/compute/cluster/jobs"
List folder content:
curl --header "Authorization: Bearer " "http://localhost:8000/filesystem/cluster/ops/ls?path="
